About the Hackathon/Ideathon

InnoVentrix MR 1.0 (2025) is a thrilling 24-hour innovation marathon powered by the Dr. O.P. Bhalla Foundation and CSI MRIIRS, uniting student developers, designers, innovators, and creative minds from across the region. While the Hackathon Track invites tech enthusiasts to collaborate on impactful, future-ready solutions, our parallel Ideathon Track opens the doors for non-technical participants to share bold ideas and fresh perspectives on real-world sustainability challenges. Together, these twin tracks create an inclusive, dynamic space where diverse talents and visions convergeâ€”fueling innovation, inspiring change, and shaping a more sustainable future.
