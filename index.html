About the Hackathon

InnoVentrix MR 1.0 is an exciting 24-hour hackathon hosted by the Dr. OP Bhalla Foundation, uniting student developers, designers, and innovators from across the region. Our mission is to empower the next generation to create impactful tech solutions for real-world sustainability challenges, carrying forward Dr. O.P. Bhalla's legacy of using innovation for the upliftment of society.

Innovate

Develop groundbreaking solutions for pressing environmental and societal issues.

Collaborate

Work in teams of 2-4, bringing diverse skills to create scalable, impactful tech ideas.

Grow

Gain hands-on experience, receive mentorship from industry experts, and network with tech leaders.

Theme: Tech for Sustainability

Our goal is to empower students to build technology-driven solutions that tackle pressing environmental and societal issues. We challenge you to align your solutions with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
